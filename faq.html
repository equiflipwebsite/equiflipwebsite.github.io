FAQs

What is Equiflip?

Equiflip is the equestrian mobile destination to buy and sell all of your new and used tack, equipment, and apparel. Shop different brands with trust and ease with just the click of a button.

How do I get started on Equiflip?

  • Create an account: Complete your profile
  • Shop different brands: Use the search feature to find your favorite brands. Check back daily to see new listings on the app.
  • Build your feed: Your feed will become a collection of listings posted by members that you choose to follow. Follow your favorite riders and tack shops to find the brands you love.
  • Start your tack trunk: List the items you would like to sell. Snap a picture, write a short description, and click to post! Your listing will then appear in your followerâ€™s feeds and will be searchable in the search function.

How do I sell on Equiflip?

Create a listing by clicking on the camera icon on the bottom bar in the app. Take a photo of your product, pick a price, write a short description, choose a category, and post your item!

What are the fees to sell on Equiflip?

Equiflip is free to download. For each sale made on the app, the seller will retain 80% of the selling price. Equiflip will make a 20% commission on each sale. Once the transaction is final and the buyer has received the item, funds will be released to the seller.

What is Equiflip return policy?

All items are final sale on Equiflip.
